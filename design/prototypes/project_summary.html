What we're building
A fully AI-powered guitar tutoring platform that adapts to every learner.
Browser-based, no download required. No human instructors — all tutoring, feedback, and content adaptation is AI-driven. The platform adjusts its language, tone, interface complexity, and feedback style in real time based on who the learner is and how they're doing.

Who we're designing for
The Absolute Beginner
Never played. Needs structure, encouragement, and zero jargon. Scared of looking stupid.
The Lapsed Player
Played years ago. Doesn't want to start from scratch. Needs a fast path back.
The Intermediate Grinder
Can play songs but has plateaued. Needs targeted feedback and a way out of the plateau.
The Advanced Learner
Near-professional. Needs depth, precision, and respect for their time.
Core features
🎙️
Practice Mode Main feature
Play into your microphone and get real-time AI feedback on pitch, timing, and chord change speed. Your tutor listens alongside you, plays the same guitar, and gives a personalised post-session summary.
🎓
AI Tutor
On-demand conversational tutor, available 24/7. Adapts tone, vocabulary, and complexity to your level and emotional state.
🎬
Lesson Player
Structured video curriculum by level and guitar type. Loop sections, slow down, toggle chord overlays.
📈
Progress Dashboard
Streak, practice time, lesson completion, and level tracking. Motivational for beginners, analytical for experts.
