# AI Guitar Tutor

A fully AI-powered online guitar tutoring platform delivering real-time feedback, pre-recorded lessons, and on-demand AI tutoring — all in a web browser, no download required.

## What it does

- **Real-time AI feedback** — play into your microphone and get instant pitch and timing analysis
- **Pre-recorded lessons** — structured curriculum across Beginner, Intermediate, and Expert levels
- **AI tutor** — on-demand conversational tutor available 24/7, aware of your progress and goals
- **Adaptive learning** — the platform adjusts content, tone, and difficulty based on your skill level, language, and emotional state
- **Three guitar types** — Acoustic, Electric, and Classical, each with tailored content and feedback

## Stack

- **Frontend:** React
- **Backend:** Node.js
- **Database:** PostgreSQL
- **AI:** Audio analysis engine, LLM-powered tutor, adaptive content engine

## Languages

English, Spanish, French
